About Suffering Jukebox

A free Silver Jews stream and Purple Mountains player for David Berman fans.

What is Suffering Jukebox?

Suffering Jukebox is an interactive online jukebox for the music of David Berman. It brings together the Silver Jews discography and the Purple Mountains album in one place so you can stream songs, explore lyrics, rate tracks, and build playlists without digging through scattered links.

The name comes from “Suffering Jukebox,” a song on Silver Jews’ final studio album Lookout Mountain, Lookout Sea (2008). This site is a fan-built tribute, not affiliated with Drag City or the Berman estate.

Purple Mountains

David Berman returned in 2019 with Purple Mountains, his first release after retiring Silver Jews. The self-titled album is included in Suffering Jukebox alongside the full Silver Jews catalog.

Why use this Silver Jews stream player?
